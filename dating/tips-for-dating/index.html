Tips for Dating

Here are a few tips to get you started on having a successful date.

Bringing up topics

Do not bring up topics you are not willing to talk about more in details. If you are engaging in a conversation the worst thing to do is bring up a topic you have no desire to elaborate on. Your date would wonder why bring up a topic and might think you have something to hide which will give you the appearance of being dishonest. Only talk about topics you are interested in talking about.

Never talk about your ex

Nobody wants to feel like they are being compared to somebody else. It would also give the impression that you have not moved on. If you’re hoping for a second date then definitely avoid talking about your ex.

Be yourself

The best policy in life is never be fake and this doesn’t apply only in the world of dating. In general always be yourself.

Make eye contact

Always make eye contact this way your date will know you are interested and a great way to establish a connection. It’s well known that the eyes are the window to the soul who knows you might find your soul mate.

Smile

Smile when appropriate, try not to fake a smile it really doesn’t look attractive.

Listen

It’s always good to listen carefully so you can seamlessly carry on conversation and ask questions. A good conversation is like a good tennis game, however keep your ears on the ball and listen.

Chivalry is still alive

Men should try to adopt being chivalrous at their best. A small gesture like holding the door open or pulling out the chair makes a lady feel special. As for the ladies don’t expect too much chivalry from your knight in shining armor as this can lead to quick judgment or disappointment but just a little chivalry is like the icing on the cake, it’s very sweet so don’t expect too much.

Stay positive 

The last thing a date wants to hear is negative comments about work colleagues, what’s happening in your life and all things drama. Leave your baggage aside as this could easily push a date away from another date.

Pick a great location

If you are going to pick a location for a date think about what you are both interested in so that you can determine what place would best suit both parties.

Splitting the bill

It’s good to at least offer to split the bill. Your date would appreciate this and won’t feel you were just looking for a free meal and makes a good impression.
