How to Take the Perfect Selfie for Your Dating Profile

Find your best angle – take selfies using all angles to find the angle that best accentuates your features.

Use different poses to get your best selfie. Use a genuine smile – try to think about something that makes you happy, nobody likes a fake smile.

Have the best lighting – you can use a bedroom lamp, or bright light even consider natural daylight for that authentic look. Stay away from fluorescent lights as they bring out imperfections. The ideal lighting for looking good in photos is indirect sunlight.

Using filters correctly - don’t over filter your selfie you want to appear how you look in person.

Choose a surrounding carefully – choose a surrounding that makes you feel comfortable as you take your selfie, you don’t want to take a selfie with a dumpster behind you.

Wear appropriate clothing – if you’re not at the beach on holiday you don’t want to wear a bikini. Find something that matches your personality, whether it’s conservative, casual, trendy or colorful.

Front face or back camera selfie – test each side for best selfie.

Try to relax – it’s just a selfie, don’t overthink it and keep it simple.
