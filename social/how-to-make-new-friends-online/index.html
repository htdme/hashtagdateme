How To Make New Friends Online

The internet is a great place for online dating, however it is now even easier to find friends online. In this article you’ll discover how to find people, connect and make friends instantly.

If you haven’t already, check out Hashtagdateme

Hashtagdateme is a smart intuitive social dating app helping you to find a date, make friends, social networking and collaborating. This article will show you step by step guide on how you can match with people for making friends near you. If you are too busy to find the right people offline you can now instantly match up with hundreds of people with interests, hobbies and lifestyle you are interested in.

 

Step 1: Download the app

Hashtagdateme is available on android and iTunes. Click here now to download app on your device.

Step 2: Quick easy sign up

Enter email and password for quick account creation. Fill in all the relevant details.

Step 3: Write About Yourself

Introduce yourself in a few words. This introduction does not need to be too long as in the next two optional boxes you can hashtag things about you and what you are looking for.

Step 4: Hashtags About You and What You Are Looking For

Next to the Hashtags about me: add keywords that describe you such as coffeelover glutenfree fun active fit bodybuilder explorer. Next to Hashtags of What You Are Looking For add keywords of interests, hobbies or qualities you are seeking. You can copy and paste hashtags from keywords about you section if you are looking for similar interest and qualities and include other keywords. Example you may enjoy #football but looking for someone who is into #yoga.
